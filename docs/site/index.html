Solace

A compassionate AI companion for mental wellness — powered by Gemma 4, running entirely on your device.

Kaggle · Gemma 4 Good Hackathon
⬇ Download v2.0.5 GitHub Repository
💬

AI Companion Chat

Natural, empathetic conversations powered by Gemma 4 E2B with 128K context, running locally on your phone.

🧗

Guided Sessions

Evidence-based therapeutic exercises for anxiety, panic attacks, sleep, daily check-ins, and crisis support.

🎤

Voice Interaction

Offline speech-to-text with Vosk and text-to-speech with KittenTTS. No cloud services required.

👁

Vision Understanding

Attach images for the AI to analyze. Powered by Gemma 4's native multimodal capabilities.

🛡

Crisis Resources

Always-accessible emergency contacts: 988 Lifeline, iCall India, Vandrevala Foundation, Crisis Text Line.

🔒

100% Private

All AI inference happens on-device. Your conversations never leave your phone. Works offline.
